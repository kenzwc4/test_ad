About our services

We are the professional agents who are experts in selling products on marketplace on Amazon.co.jp. Our staff worked at Amazon Japan so that they have better understanding of Amazon platform than anyone else to sell more products on the platform.

We have supported more than 1,000 retailers to start their business on Amazon.co.jp. providing professional support for listing, fulfillment, SEO, Customer service and more. Most of our clients succeeded in selling more items within a short time period and continuously growing their business.

About Amazon.co.jp

Amazon.co.jp is rapidly growing online platform that has $13B sales and 45MM customers. All the user interfaces are identical with other Amazon platform and intuitive. For Sellers at Amazon.com, you can mange your account for daily operation just like you do at Seller Central that you are already familiar with. We will of course give necessary support if needed.

Easy 5 steps to launch your store at Amazon.co.jp

  1. Online registration entry
  2. Open your account at Amazon.co.jp
  3. List your products onto Amazon.co.jp
  4. Send your products to Amazon Fulfillment center in Japan.
  5. Start selling when products are properly received and ready for ship.
